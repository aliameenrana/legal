Privacy Policy

FLIP! - Gravity Runner

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how FLIP! ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Display Name: Optional name for leaderboards (stored locally on your device)
  • Consent Preferences: Your privacy choices (stored locally)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

If you consent to full data collection, we may collect:

  • Game Analytics: Session duration, distances traveled, scores, gameplay events (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, screen resolution, language
  • Advertising Data: Advertising ID (for personalized ads), ad interaction data
  • Crash Reports: App crash logs and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
Essential Only Mode: If you choose "Essential Only" at the consent screen, we collect no analytics, no advertising data, and no crash reports. Only data required for the game to function is stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience
  • To show advertisements (personalized if you consent, non-personalized otherwise)
  • To analyze game performance and fix bugs
  • To track gameplay statistics for leaderboards (local only)
  • To send push notifications (only if you grant permission)

4. Third-Party Services

If you consent to full data collection, the app may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob / AppLovin MAX — Advertising (Google Privacy Policy, AppLovin Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Analytics — Usage analytics (Firebase Privacy)
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Crash reporting
  • Google Play Games Services — Leaderboards and achievements (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Unity — Game engine telemetry (Unity Privacy Policy)

5. Advertising

The app displays ads to support free gameplay:

  • Banner Ads: Shown during gameplay
  • Interstitial Ads: Shown between game sessions (limited frequency)
  • Rewarded Ads: Optional — watch to earn in-game rewards (revives, gems)

If you accept full consent, ads may be personalized based on your interests. If you choose "Essential Only", ads will be non-personalized.

You can purchase "Remove Ads" as an in-app purchase to disable banner and interstitial ads.

6. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases for virtual currency and cosmetic items. All purchase transactions are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

7. Children's Privacy

FLIP! is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Storage and Security

  • Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs
  • No account creation is required to play the game
  • Analytics data (if consented) is processed by third-party services listed in Section 4
  • We do not operate our own servers for data storage

9. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw Consent: Clear app data or reinstall to reset your consent preferences
  • Access Your Data: Contact us to request information about data collected
  • Delete Your Data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. For analytics data held by third parties, contact us and we will facilitate deletion
  • Opt Out: Choose "Essential Only" at the consent screen, or reset your Advertising ID in your device settings

10. Push Notifications

The app may request permission to send push notifications for:

  • Daily challenge reminders
  • Login streak alerts
  • Game updates and events

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
